# Scoring Rubric

Use a 1–5 score.

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Poor / structurally weak |
| 2 | Usable but problematic |
| 3 | Adequate |
| 4 | Strong |
| 5 | Excellent / strategically advantaged |

## Confidence

| Confidence | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Low | Limited evidence or high uncertainty |
| Medium | Plausible with some evidence |
| High | Strong evidence or clear structural basis |

## Required fields

Every evaluation must include:

- score
- confidence
- justification
- at least one supporting claim, once claims exist
